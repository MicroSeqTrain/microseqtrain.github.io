MicroRunQC

MicroRunQC is a workflow in GalaxyTrakr that takes raw fastq files, runs the tools Trimmomatic and microrunqc, and concatenates and filters the resulting output into collections of draft de novo assemblies, quality assessments, and sequencing types. The latter are contained in tabular data files for download. The protocol linked below in the References section covers use of the Workflow in detail.
