This tutorial will show you how to create a collection of paired forward and reverse fastq files in GalaxyTrakr.

Coming soon!

This tutorial assumes that you have already imported fastq foward and reverse reads into a GalaxyTrakr history. If you need assistance with that task, please see this tutorial.

  1. With the history containing your unpaired fastq files active, navigate to the GalaxyTrakr main page and look for the checkmark button beneath your history's name. Click it, and check the resulting boxes to the left of the fastq files you would like to pair.
  2. Click For all selected... button and select Build List of Dataset Pairs.
  3. In the resulting pane, you will see two dropdown boxes containing suffixes that your fastq files may have to indicate whether they are the forward or reverse file. Select the appropriate suffix from the dropdown. you You should see the fastq files you selected to pair autopopulate in the pane with GalaxyTrakr matching the files into pairs based on their names.
  4. If the pairs of files you see are correct and no files are listed that you do not want to be paired, click Auto-pair. This will result in the pairs of files and the name that will be given to the subsequent pair in the box beneath where the original list of files was. If you see that any files in that list have not moved down to be paired, check for naming errors or missing files.

    5. Before you accept these pairings, we would suggest deselecting the checkbox that reads Hide original elements? so that you can better track the origin of the pairs in your history or copy them individually to other histories, if you wish.

  5. Give your collection of pairs a name.
  6. Click the Create collection button.
