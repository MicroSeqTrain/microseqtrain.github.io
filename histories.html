GalaxyTrakr Basics: Histories

This tutorial documents various ways you can make Histories, a means of organizing your data and work in GalaxyTrakr, work for you. Histories are a container for your data and the ways you have worked with it. They can be searched, copied, deleted, and purged permanently. Similarly, all of those actions can also be performed on the individual items within those histories. This video tutorial will show you how to perform those tasks.
